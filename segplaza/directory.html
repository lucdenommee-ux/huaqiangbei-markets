🧭 Zone-Based Navigation

📱 West Zone (W) – iPhone Wholesale Heart

If you're looking for iPhones (Grade A/B/C), head straight to the West Zone on floors 2 and 3. This is where the highest concentration of iPhone wholesalers operates. You'll find the best bulk prices and the widest selection of colors/capacities.

🚪 South Entrance – Direct Access to Escalators

The South Entrance (facing Huaqiang North Road) gives you the fastest access to the escalators going up to floors 2–4. Ideal if you want to skip the crowded ground floor and go straight to the mobile core.

🔧 East Zone (E) – Android Parts & Repair

For Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus spare parts, or professional repair tools, the East Zone on floor 4 is your destination. Specialists in OLED screens, batteries, and micro‑soldering equipment.

📊 Premium (L2) vs Export/Wholesale (L3)

FloorTypeWhat to Expect
2nd FloorPremium / Grade A+Pristine condition devices (like new), individual boxes, higher prices. Best for clients who demand perfection.
3rd FloorExport / Wholesale (Grades B/C)Functional devices with cosmetic wear, sold in bulk trays (5–10 units). Lower prices, ideal for resellers and emerging markets.

💡 Luc's Advice: "If you need 10+ units, skip L2 and go directly to L3. You'll save 15–20% per device."
