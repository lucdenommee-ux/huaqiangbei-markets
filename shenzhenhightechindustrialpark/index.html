Shenzhen High-Tech Industrial Park (SHIP)

深圳高新技术产业园区 · Nanshan District, Shenzhen

National-level science and technology industrial park, birthplace of Tencent, ZTE, and DJI.

📍 Nanshan District, Shenzhen (Keyuan Road South / Keji Road South) 🚇 Hi-Tech Park Station (Line 1) - Exit C or D 🏢 Established September 1996 · 11.52 km²

📍 Overview / 概览

The Shenzhen High-Tech Industrial Park (SHIP) is a 11.52 km² national-level science and technology industrial park established in September 1996.

深圳高新技术产业园区成立于1996年9月，规划面积11.52平方公里。

🚀 "China's Silicon Valley"

🏢 Key Tenants / 主要企业

Tencent, ZTE, DJI, Mindray, Tsinghua University Research Institute, and over 6,000 tech companies.

腾讯、中兴、大疆、迈瑞、清华研究院等6000多家科技企业。

🔬 60+ university branches

🚇 Access / 交通

Metro: Hi-Tech Park Station (Line 1) - Exit C or D

Location: Nanshan District, Keyuan Road South / Keji Road South

地铁1号线高新园站C/D出口 · 南山区科苑南路/科技南路

🚀 Innovation Ecosystem / 创新生态

SHIP is one of the six key national science and technology parks in China. It houses the Shenzhen Virtual University Park and numerous R&D centers.

国家级六大科技园区之一，拥有深圳虚拟大学园及众多研发中心。
